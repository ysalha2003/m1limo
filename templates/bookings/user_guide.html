Booking a Ride

Trip Types

Point-to-Point

Single destination trips with optional intermediate stops.

Round Trip

Outbound and return legs scheduled together with linked management.

Hourly Service

Reserve a vehicle for a specified duration for multiple destinations.

Booking Process

  1. Select Trip Details: Choose trip type, pickup/drop-off locations, date, and time. For airport transfers, include flight numbers for real-time tracking.
  2. Choose Vehicle: Select from Sedans (2 passengers), SUVs (6 passengers), or Sprinter Vans (12 passengers) based on your party size.
  3. Passenger Information: Provide passenger name and contact details. You can select from saved frequent passengers or enter new information.
  4. Special Requests: Add any specific requirements, accessibility needs, or notes for the chauffeur.
  5. Review & Submit: Confirm details and submit your reservation for confirmation.
Pro Tip:

Save frequent passengers in your profile for one-click selection on future reservations. Perfect for recurring trips or team travel.

Reservation Confirmation

Once submitted, you'll receive an email confirmation with your reservation reference number. Most reservations are confirmed within minutes during business hours.

Status Types: Pending (awaiting confirmation) → Confirmed (approved and scheduled) → Trip Completed (service rendered)

Managing Your Reservations

Dashboard Overview

Your dashboard displays all reservations with filtering options by status, date, and search criteria. View upcoming trips, track past services, and access reservation details instantly.

Editing Reservations

Confirmed reservations can be edited up to 2 hours before pickup. This allows you to adjust:

  • Pickup or drop-off addresses
  • Passenger name and contact information
  • Number of passengers
  • Special requests or notes

Important: Vehicle type and trip dates cannot be changed once confirmed. For major modifications, cancel and create a new reservation.

Cancellation Policy

Reservations can be cancelled with the following terms:

  • Pending Bookings: Free cancellation if more than 4 hours before pickup; full charges if within 4 hours
  • Confirmed Bookings: Free cancellation if more than 2 hours before pickup; full charges if within 2 hours
Best Practice:

Cancel as early as possible if your plans change to avoid charges and help us reallocate resources efficiently.

Round Trip Management

Round trips consist of linked outbound and return legs. You can:

  • Edit each leg independently (up to 2 hours before respective pickup)
  • Cancel both trips together or just one leg
  • View combined trip details in your dashboard
