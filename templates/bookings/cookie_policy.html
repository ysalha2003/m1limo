Cookie Policy

Last Updated: January 2025

Summary: M1 Limousine uses essential cookies to provide basic functionality. We do not use tracking or advertising cookies.

What Are Cookies

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit our website. They help the website remember your preferences and session information.

Cookies We Use

Essential Cookies (Required)

These cookies are necessary for the website to function:

  • Session Cookie: Maintains your login session while using the reservation platform
  • CSRF Token: Security measure to protect against cross-site request forgery

These cookies cannot be disabled as they are essential for the platform to work properly.

Cookies We Do NOT Use

M1 Limousine does not use:

  • Analytics or tracking cookies
  • Advertising cookies
  • Third-party marketing cookies
  • Social media cookies

Managing Cookies

You can control cookies through your browser settings. However, disabling essential cookies will prevent you from logging in and using the reservation system.

Most browsers allow you to:

  • View and delete cookies
  • Block cookies from specific websites
  • Block all cookies
  • Clear all cookies when closing the browser

Contact

If you have questions about our use of cookies, contact us at mo@m1limo.com.
